Privacy Policy

Sticker Hero: Idle RPG

Effective Date: July 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sticker Hero: Idle RPG ("Sticker Hero," "we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe game experience.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our game, online account features, global chat, cloud save, advertisements, and purchase services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Most gameplay can be used locally on your device. If you use online features, we may collect and store:

  • Online account identifiers, device identifiers used for sign-in, and authentication tokens
  • Your chosen username/display name
  • Your highest reported player level and basic game progress used for chat access or cloud save summaries
  • Global chat messages, reports, moderation actions, blocked player records, timestamps, and safety logs
  • Cloud backup save data if you choose to upload or enable cloud backups
  • Gmail/Google account email address if you choose to link Google sign-in
  • Purchase, entitlement, and refund/voided-purchase verification records
  • Basic server logs needed for security, abuse prevention, debugging, and service reliability

For global chat, we use a neutral age gate. We do not need or store your full birthdate for chat; we only store whether the chat age gate was passed or whether free-text chat should remain locked.

Third-party services used in our app may also collect data as described below.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our app. Google AdMob may collect the following information:

  • Device information (device type, OS version, unique identifiers)
  • Ad interaction data
  • Advertising ID
  • Usage data and analytics

Google processes this data according to its own Privacy Policy.

3.2 Google Play Billing

We use Google Play Billing to process in-app purchases. Google Play may collect:

  • Purchase history
  • Payment information (processed securely by Google)
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage data

Google processes payment data according to Google's Privacy Policy and the Google Play Terms of Service.

3.3 Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Gmail/Google, Google may provide us with an account identifier, email address, and profile name so we can connect your Sticker Hero online account and cloud save. Google processes this data according to its own Privacy Policy.

4. Local Data Storage and Cloud Save

Your game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. Local data:

  • Remains on your device unless you choose to use online backup features
  • Remains on your device under your control
  • Will be deleted if you uninstall the app or clear your app data

If you enable or manually use cloud backup, your save backup code and summary information may be uploaded to our server so you can restore progress on another device or after reinstalling.

5. Global Chat and User-Generated Content

Global chat is optional and is not required to play Sticker Hero. To send free-text global chat messages, players must choose a unique username, reach the required game level, pass the chat age gate, and accept the in-game chat consent/rules.

Chat messages are public to other players. Do not share personal information, real names, addresses, contact details, school/work details, passwords, links, or anything private.

We may filter, block, hide, review, delete, or moderate chat messages and usernames. Players can report messages and block other players. Repeated or serious violations may result in temporary mutes, bans, deleted messages, or other account restrictions.

6. How We Use Information

We use information collected (directly and through third parties) to:

  • Provide and improve our app and services
  • Display personalized advertisements (via Google AdMob)
  • Process in-app purchases (via Google Play Billing)
  • Verify purchases, detect refunds/voided purchases, and manage entitlements or gem debt
  • Provide online accounts, Gmail sign-in, global chat, moderation, reporting, blocking, and cloud save features
  • Analyze usage patterns and improve user experience
  • Protect players, prevent spam, enforce chat rules, and maintain service security
  • Comply with legal obligations

7. Data Security

We take data security seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However:

  • No transmission over the internet is 100% secure
  • We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data
  • You use our services at your own risk

Third-party services (Google AdMob, Google Play, and Google Sign-In) maintain their own security standards and practices.

8. Children's Privacy

Sticker Hero may be suitable for a general audience, but free-text global chat is not available to players who select that they are under 13 or otherwise not permitted to use online chat in their country/region.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without required consent. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information through online features, we will take reasonable steps to delete such information or disable the relevant online access.
